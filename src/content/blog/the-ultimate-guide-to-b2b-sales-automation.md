## What Is B2B Sales Automation?

In general, sales automation automates repetitive and mundane manual tasks. The purpose of sales automation is to save sales teams time and money.

Sales automation is generally done in a composite software system called Customer Relationship Management (CRM). For a sales team, automation is required in data driven processes like lead generation, quoting, invoicing, outreach software and others.

In specific industries, specific sections of sales workflow may be required, like inventory management and project management for manufacturer’s reps in the HVAC industry. Therefore, sales automation automates any section or the whole sales cycle using sales enablement tools and software.

## Top 5 Examples of Sales Automation

Sales is the most crucial part of a business. Without any success in sales, there is no growth in the company. Therefore, companies invest their time and money in upskilling their sales and marketing teams. Sales automation is one of the investments that can significantly improve the sales cycles of a business and thus grow a company faster.

Automation in a sales cycle can be done in all parts of the cycle or just one or two parts based on the needs and requirements of the company. Some of the sections of a sales cycle that are automated are as follows:

#### Lead Management Automation

Lead management is first stage of the sales cycle. Lead management involves lead generation, capturing, nurturing, and conversion. The entire lead cycle starts from capturing the lead to converting the lead into a customer. Therefore, it is the most sought-after sales automation tool.

Lead management automation is an example of B2B sales automation that can significantly impact. By automating lead capture and qualification, sales teams can focus on the most promising leads, improving conversion rates, and shortening the sales cycle.

Many B2B sales automation platforms can automate the entire lead cycle, from capturing leads on your website to nurturing them through email campaigns and social media.

These platforms can track lead engagement with your content and website and assign lead scores to identify the most promising leads for your sales team to pursue.

#### Automation in Sales Engagement

Sales engagement involves prospecting, nurturing the leads and one-to-one engagement with hot leads. The results of sales success are immediate and therefore they show direct growth.

By automating sales engagement process like follow-ups with prospects, digitizing the contacts history and related information on a single platform can help optimize the engagement with customers and prospects.

Sales engagement tools like [Orgzit](https://orgzit.com/) can help sales reps to track engagement with their outreach efforts.

Personalized video emails are an example of sales engagement automation that can help engage prospects. Sales reps can use Vidyard to create and send personalized video messages to their prospects, increasing engagement rates and building a stronger connection with potential customers.

These video messages can also be tracked, allowing sales reps to see who has viewed their content and follow up accordingly.

#### Contract Management Automation

On an average, a small business has to manage more than ten contracts at the same time weekly or daily. Manual contract management or through scattered spreadsheets or Excel can lead to errors and delays. It may cause frustration to the salesperson and thus disappointment to the customer.

Contract management automation is a type of B2B sales automation that can streamline the contract process. The contract process includes drafting a contract, negotiation, status of the negotiation, comments on negotittaion from managers, signing of the contract with the customers and storing it.

E-signature software like DocuSign or Adobe Sign can digitize the signature process, automate the contract signing process, and reduce the time and effort required to complete contracts. This can help companies close deals faster and improve the overall customer experience.

#### Quotation Management Software

Once the prospect is ready to buy, you need to quote the prices to your customer. This is like a first impression of how your company works with its customers. A quick, accurate quote can make or break the deal for you. A professional-looking quote can improve customer satisfaction enormously.

  
With automation of quote generation you can pull up product price, specifications, your logo, address, customer information, and other related things without so much as blinking your eyes. A quote will be generated within few seconds speeding up the whole process of quoting. Apart from generating quote, you can follow-up with customers, make revisions, get quote approval all throught the automated platform.

Creating automatic quotes with quote management software like [Orgzit CRM](https://orgzit.com/quotation-management-software/) can really save your time and generate accurate quotes.

#### Invoice Management

Once the quote is approved after several follow-ups and approval through the management, it is time for invoicing. Now the finance or accounting departments will create an invoice for the customer. Now, it is important to apply the discounts and margins correctly. Manual processes are prone to errors. However, an automated platform can calculate the discounts and margins that you have set for the product and services and apply them to the invoices and generate it quickly.

It is a tedious but very important task, or your company would never get paid. Automation for such a thing is rather easy to accept as they arent something that need a human touch. They are just invoices, simple and plain.  
  
In a unified platform like Orgzit, finance or accounting team can collaborate with sales and quoting team while generating invoice. ALl this is possible through single platform and without back and forth meetings. Paperless communication without having to communicate separately with each time.
