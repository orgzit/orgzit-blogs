## FAQs

**Why can’t I just use a CRM designed for large companies?**  
Many SMB owners wonder if they can adapt enterprise-level CRMs to suit their needs. The truth is, that these CRMs are often over-complicated for small businesses and come with unnecessary features that increase costs without adding value.

**What should I look for in a CRM as a small business owner?**  
Small business owners need a CRM that’s simple to implement, flexible, and scalable as their needs evolve. Features like user-friendly interfaces, customizable workflows, and the ability to manage simple data efficiently are key.

**How quickly can I implement a custom CRM for my business?**  
Most small businesses are concerned with time and want a quick setup. A custom CRM can often be implemented in a much shorter timeframe than a large, traditional system, allowing your business to start benefiting from it sooner.
