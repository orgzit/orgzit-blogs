## **Benefits of CRM**

![Benefits of CRM](/blog/blog-images/4.jpg)

Managing customer details like emails and contacts in rows and columns as sales funnel stage can be tiring and erring.

A CRM system tracks _customer interactions_ (the leads and prospects) across business cycles for you. This mechanism acts as an effective tool to expand and manage their deliverance. So let’s discover the benefits of CRM:

### **Customer Tracking**

_Accenture’s study titled ”B2B Customer Experience Survey” revealed that more than 8 in 10 B2B executives recognize CX as a strategic priority._

![Customer Tracking](/blog/blog-images/5-1.png)

CRM has an ecosystem that provides customer history and your journey with them. You can analyze & track the growth and the present status of all your clients and leads. This way, you can also track various domains through records and history.

### **Catch-all leads**

![Generate Leads](/blog/blog-images/6.jpg)

With a CRM tracking system, you can create web-to-lead forms and embed them on your contact pages on your website.

The captured information is automatically logged to your CRM and assigned to the appropriate salesperson, which allows for a quick follow-up, so you don’t lose sales!

### **Enhances communication**

![Enhances Communication](/blog/blog-images/7-1.png)

It [automates](https://orgzit.com/product/) many mundane tasks such as data entry, team-department communication, _tracking customer interactions,_ and cloud backup. Consequently, sales, marketing, and customer service teams can make timely, informed decisions on upselling to cross-selling.

### **All in one tool**

![All in one CRM](/blog/blog-images/8-1.jpg)

CRM is beneficial because it helps manage both mammoth and meager tasks.

For instance, you can send emails & links to prospects and, mention customer accounts and their activities, and frame campaigns as prerequisites.  
Real-time CRM management regularly keeps inventory, prices, and transaction data up to date. A sales team with access to the latest data can provide accurate and updated [quotes](https://orgzit.com/quotation-management-software/) to customers. If any change occurs in the quote, CRM modifies it accordingly; that way, clients are not deluded.

### **A storehouse of customer data**

![Storehouse of Customer Data](/blog/blog-images/9.png)

A CRM tool ensures secure and unlimited storage of data. It means that you can store all the details of your clients in one place and use it instantly when needed. It provides a cloud backup to keep customers’ records safe (in case you miss a file or PC crash, you’ll always have a tie-up with CRM).

### **Automated instant Customer Status**

![Automated instant customer status](/blog/blog-images/10.png)

With efficient CRM, you can easily decipher the customer status and the level of involvement with your venture. This way, you can always link the sales and marketing departments to cater to your business with potential and current customers (for instance, those who are willing to make a demo call).

You can monitor what your customer wants through this _customer tracking_ software and assist the [workflow management](https://orgzit.com/workflow-management/). Through selective data sharing, you can access the sales team to the same resources to capture the potential prospect.

### **Know your numbers**

Another significant benefit of CRM is that your sales team can be informed about how close they are to achieving their individual monthly, quarterly, and annual goals through weekly emails!

### **Analytics and Reporting**

![Analytics & Reporting](/blog/blog-images/11.jpg)

In B2B/B2C business, if you want to know the success rate of a marketing campaign or want to calculate the ROI, CRM software is the answer.

It quickly creates reliable reports based on your business metrics like profitability, sales forecasts, conversions, and goal progress.

### **Customized Campaign**

![Customized Campaign](/blog/blog-images/12.png)

It can be a mammoth task if you have many product lines or want to run different campaigns simultaneously. Isn’t it? However, if you have a reliable CRM, you can create highly personalized and automated campaigns for target customers.

You can shift through the top-of-the-funnel leads or identify the potentially profitable customers. Based on their buying preferences and behavior, you can segment them into groups and send focused communication.
